# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# Purpose

This Product Requirements Document (PRD) defines the functional, technical, and user experience requirements for the Lauren Palace Guest House Platform.

The purpose of this document is to serve as the primary blueprint for designing, developing, testing, and deploying the platform.

Unlike the previous documentation, which focused on research and strategy, this document specifies exactly what will be built.

Every page, feature, component, workflow, database entity, API, and user interaction should be defined here before implementation begins.

This document will guide the development process and provide GitHub Copilot with a detailed understanding of the product vision and expected functionality.

---

# Vision

Lauren Palace Guest House Platform will provide guests with a premium, secure, and seamless direct booking experience that rivals leading hospitality platforms while reflecting the warmth, professionalism, and hospitality of Lauren Palace.

The platform will allow guests to:

- Discover the guest house.
- Explore rooms.
- Check availability.
- Book accommodation.
- Make secure payments.
- Manage reservations.
- Communicate with staff.
- Leave reviews.
- Return for future stays.

At the same time, the platform will provide management with powerful tools for operating the guest house efficiently through a modern administration dashboard.

# 1. Product Overview

## Introduction

Lauren Palace Guest House Platform is a modern hospitality platform designed to provide guests with a seamless direct booking experience while giving management complete control over reservations, payments, guest communication, and day-to-day operations.

Unlike traditional brochure websites, the platform will function as a complete digital ecosystem that combines marketing, booking, payment processing, customer relationship management, and property administration into a single unified solution.

The platform will be accessible from desktop, tablet, and mobile devices and will prioritise speed, simplicity, accessibility, and trust throughout every interaction.

---

## Problem Statement

Lauren Palace Guest House currently relies heavily on third-party booking platforms to receive reservations.

While these platforms provide visibility and attract new guests, they introduce several challenges:

- Commission fees reduce revenue from every booking.
- The Lauren Palace brand becomes secondary to the booking platform.
- Guest relationships are owned by third-party services.
- Marketing opportunities are limited.
- Returning guests often book through intermediaries instead of directly.
- The booking experience cannot be fully customised.

Lauren Palace requires an independent platform that enables guests to discover, trust, and book directly through the official website.

---

## Solution

The Lauren Palace Guest House Platform will provide an end-to-end booking experience that allows guests to:

- Learn about the guest house.
- Browse accommodation options.
- View live availability.
- Compare room types.
- Make secure online payments.
- Receive instant booking confirmation.
- Manage upcoming reservations.
- Contact the guest house directly.
- Leave verified reviews after their stay.

For management, the platform will provide tools to manage rooms, bookings, payments, guests, reporting, and operational tasks through a secure administration dashboard.

---

## Primary Objectives

The platform has five primary objectives.

### Increase Direct Bookings

Encourage guests to book through the official Lauren Palace website rather than third-party platforms.

---

### Deliver an Exceptional Guest Experience

Provide an intuitive booking journey that is simple, fast, trustworthy, and enjoyable.

---

### Improve Operational Efficiency

Reduce manual administration through automated booking management, payment tracking, and guest communication.

---

### Strengthen the Lauren Palace Brand

Position Lauren Palace as a premium accommodation provider with a professional online presence.

---

### Support Future Growth

Design the platform with scalability in mind so that additional guest houses, properties, or hospitality services can be added in the future without redesigning the system.

## Success Metrics

The success of Version 1 of the platform will be measured using key performance indicators (KPIs), including:

- Increase in direct bookings.
- Reduction in third-party booking commissions.
- Website conversion rate.
- Booking completion rate.
- Average booking value.
- Guest satisfaction.
- Average review score.
- Repeat guest rate.
- Mobile performance.
- Website loading speed.
- Payment success rate.
- Customer support response time.

- AI concierge.
- Digital room keys.
- Loyalty programme.
- Gift vouchers.
- Multi-property management.
- Corporate booking portal.
- Live chat support.
